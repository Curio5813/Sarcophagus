Recent Games Reviews

9.3
Prince of Persia

Prince of Persia was one of the first games to feature fluid isometric graphics and detailed animations for its time, revolutionizing the video game industry. Originally released in 1989, the game tells the story of a prince who must escape from a dungeon after being betrayed by his twin brother.

9.5
The Legend of Zelda

One of the most influential games of all time, The Legend of Zelda introduced a non-linear, open world where the player was free to explore and discover new challenges. The Zelda series remains a mainstay for Nintendo to this day.

9.1
bad mojo

Bad Mojo was a rather peculiar point-and-click adventure game, originally released for DOS in 1996. The story revolves around a man who, after being fired and abandoned by his girlfriend, turns into a giant cockroach and finds himself trapped in the sewers of a large city.

9.7
Grim Fandango

Grim Fandango is a true gem among point-and-click adventure games. Originally released in 1998 by LucasArts, the game takes us to a Mexican afterlife where recently deceased souls embark on a long train journey to heaven.
